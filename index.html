FAQS

MaAvatar is a blockchain-based virtual metaverse game that allows players to raise and take care of realistic, NFT-based virtual children. It also offers immersive gameplay with endless possibilities.

MaAvatar offers realistic NFT avatars, the ability to breed with celebrity avatars, and the opportunity to raise a virtual family in the MaAvatar metaverse. It also offers monetization opportunities for both players and businesses.

Players can breed with celebrity avatars or with their partner's attributes to create a unique and realistic virtual child. Players can then take care of the virtual child by engaging in various in-game activities and playing mini-games.

The MaAvatar token is the in-game currency that players can use to purchase virtual items, in-game experiences and other features within the game. It can also be traded on various NFT marketplaces.
